# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## 1. Track Order Status
You can track your order using the tracking link sent to your email after shipping. Delivery usually takes 3–5 business days.

## 2. Refund & Returns
We offer a 7–day return and refund policy for all orders. To request a return, contact support with your order ID.

## 3. Escalation
If you are not satisfied with the response, your issue will be escalated to a support specialist within 24 hours.

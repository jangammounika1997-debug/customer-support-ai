## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1) How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You will receive a reset link by email.

### 2) How can I check the status of my order?
Go to the Orders section in your profile. Enter your order ID to see the latest status.

### 3) My order has not arrived yet. What should I do?
Sorry for the inconvenience! Please share your order number so I can check. If the product is delayed, I’ll escalate the request to our support team.

### 4) How do I update my billing address?
Go to Account > Billing Information and update your address. If you face any issue, I will raise a support ticket for you.

### 5) I want to return my product. What is the process?
You can request a return within 7 days of delivery. Go to Orders → Select product → Click "Return".

### 6) What is your customer care phone number?
Our support line is available 24/7. Contact: +91 XXXXX XXXXX.

### 7) Can I cancel my order?
Yes, if the order has not been shipped yet. Go to Your Orders → Cancel.

### 8) I made a payment but did not receive a confirmation email.
Please provide the payment transaction ID and I’ll verify it. If needed, I will escalate the issue.

### 9) How long does a refund take?
Refunds take 5–7 business days once processed.

### 10) Can I talk to a human support agent?
Sure — I will escalate your request to a live agent. Please wait while I connect you.
